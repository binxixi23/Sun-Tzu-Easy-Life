# 🏛️ Sun Tzu_Easy_Life: The Master Framework Manual

## 1. Philosophical Foundation
The "Easy Life" is not a life without challenges, but a life where challenges are solved through **Positioning (Thế)** rather than **Friction (Lực)**. This framework uses the 13 Chapters of Sun Tzu to automate decision-making across all human domains.

## 2. The Five Pillars of Life (Ngũ Sự)
Every query processed by the Agents is mapped to these five pillars:
- **Đạo (The Way):** Ethics, Family, Love, and Social Harmony.
- **Thiên (The Weather):** Health, Cycles, Timing, and Natural Laws.
- **Địa (The Terrain):** Business, Politics, Infrastructure, and Physical Space.
- **Tướng (The Command):** Discipline, Habits, Time Management, and Leadership.
- **Pháp (The Law):** Wealth, Systems, Rituals, and Technical Protocols.

## 3. Agent Orchestration Workflow
1. **The Scout (`scout_agent.py`):** Performs reconnaissance on the user's intent. Identifies the "Terrain" (Domain) and "Climate" (Urgency).
2. **The Strategist (`strategist_agent.py`):** Cross-references the Scout's data with the `data/tactical_library.md`. 
3. **The Visualizer (`visualizer_agent.py`):** Generates Mermaid.js flowcharts to reveal the "Hidden Path."
4. **The Banter Agent (`banter_agent.py`):** Maintains the user's "Spirit" (Khí) through strategic humor and parables.

## 4. Operational Commands
The system can be triggered via `scripts/generate_report.py`.
- **Primary Input:** Natural language description of a problem.
- **Output:** A Victory Plan including tactical moves, visual maps, and psychological grounding.

## 5. Domain-Specific Protocols

### 🌿 Nature & Growth (Cây cối & Thiên nhiên)
- **Strategy:** "Do not fight the seasons."
- **Execution:** Align planting or project launches with the natural energy cycles of the year.

### 🏢 Office & Politics (Chính trị & Công việc)
- **Strategy:** "Be as impenetrable as night."
- **Execution:** Use information superiority (The Scout) to anticipate reorganizations or shifts in power.

### 💰 Wealth & Provision (Tiền bạc & Vật chất)
- **Strategy:** "Supply lines are the lifeblood of the campaign."
- **Execution:** Prioritize cash flow and defensive savings before attacking high-risk investments.
