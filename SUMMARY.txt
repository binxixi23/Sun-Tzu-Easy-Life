[ EXECUTIVE SUMMARY ]
The Sun Tzu_Easy_Life framework is a Multi-Agent AI system designed to act 
as a "Digital Sovereign." It removes the friction of daily life by applying 
the 13 Chapters of Binh Pháp Tôn Tử to 25+ human domains, ranging from 
Office Politics and Wealth Management to Health and Family Ethics.

[ THE ARMORY (The Data) ]
- 13-Chapter Tactical Library: A full translation of military strategy 
  into modern "Life Hacks."
- 1,000+ Modern Scenarios: A massive case-study database for the agents 
  to cross-reference real-world problems.
- Sovereign Manifestos: Deep-dive strategic guides on the fusion of 
  Politics, Wealth, and Human Nature.

[ THE GENERAL STAFF (The Agents) ]
- THE SCOUT (Intelligence): Detects the 'Terrain' and 'Climate' of your problems.
- THE STRATEGIST (Core): Maps your situation to the 13 Pillars of Strategy.
- THE VISUALIZER (Signal): Draws Mermaid.js battle maps to reveal the path.
- THE BANTER SAGE (Spirit): Maintains your morale with witty parables and 
  philosophical humor.

[ TECHNICAL ECOSYSTEM ]
- Automated DevOps: GitHub Workflows (.github/) ensure the strategy is 
  never broken.
- Modular Architecture: Clean folder structures (core/, agents/, data/) 
  allow for infinite knowledge expansion.
- Direct-to-Markdown Reporting: Generates professional "Victory Reports" 
  in seconds.

[ FINAL DOCTRINE ]
"In the Easy Life Academy, we do not work harder; we position better. 
We do not fight; we flow. We do not struggle; we win."
