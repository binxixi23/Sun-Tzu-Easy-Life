# The System of Law (Pháp)

### 1. Financial Fortification
*   **The Golden Rule:** "Expenses must never outrun the supply lines."
*   **Compounding:** This is the "Thế" (Momentum) of wealth. A small amount, consistently applied, becomes an unstoppable force over time.

### 2. Music and Ritual (Harmony)
*   **Ritual:** Rituals (Nghi Thức) are the "Software of Society." They automate respect so you don't have to negotiate every interaction.
*   **Music:** Sound influences the "Spirit" (Khí). Use high-frequency music to rally the troops and low-frequency music to calm the mind after battle.
