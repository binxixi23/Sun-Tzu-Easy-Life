# The Celestial Cycle (Thiên)

### 1. The Circadian Warfare (Health)
*   **Day/Night:** Sleep is "Retreating to the Fortress." Attacking your work while exhausted is "Fighting uphill."
*   **Seasons:** Align your energy with nature. Winter is for "Consolidation" (rest/saving); Spring is for "Expansion" (new projects).

### 2. Life, Death, and the Void
*   **Principle:** "Change is the only constant."
*   **Strategy:** View "Sinh Lão Bệnh Tử" (Birth, Age, Sickness, Death) not as enemies, but as the "Changing Terrain." To fear death is to have a flawed scout report. Accept the cycle to move with ease.
