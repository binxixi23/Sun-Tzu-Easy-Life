# ⚔️ Tactical Cheat Sheet: Conquering Procrastination
**Agent:** Tuong_Management (The Internal Commander)
**Objective:** Total annihilation of "The Delaying Rebel."

### I. The Enemy’s Formation: "The Wall of Resistance"
Procrastination happens when the "Terrain" of the task looks too steep. 


| Sun Tzu Concept | Procrastination Tactic |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Divide & Conquer** | Break a "10-hour Siege" into "Ten 1-hour Skirmishes." |
| **The First Strike** | The "5-Minute Rule." Commit to only 5 minutes. Once the army moves, inertia is broken. |
| **Clear Orders** | Ambiguity leads to desertion. Give yourself "Specific Commands" (e.g., "Write 300 words" vs "Work on essay"). |

### II. Deploying the "Four Disciplines"
1. **The Spirit of the Morning:** Attack your hardest task when your "Khí" (Energy) is highest. If you wait until dusk, your troops are exhausted.
2. **The Burning of the Ships:** Delete social media apps during work hours. Create a "Death Ground" where you have nothing to do *but* the task.
3. **The Scout’s Reward:** Give yourself a "Spoil of War" (a break or treat) ONLY after a successful maneuver.
4. **The Silence of the General:** Turn off notifications. A general who is constantly interrupted by "pigeons" (pings) cannot see the battlefield.

### III. The Emergency Maneuver (When Paralyzed)
*   **Tactics:** If the task feels impossible, find the smallest "Weak Point" (e.g., just open the document and name it). 
*   **Mantra:** *"A march of a thousand miles begins with a single step into the fray."*
