# The Đạo of Human Connection

> "He who lacks harmony at home cannot command a legion."

### 1. The Strategy of Soft Power (Love)
*   **Principle:** "Overcome the hard with the soft."
*   **Tactics:** In conflict with a partner, do not meet fire with fire. Use "The Empty Vessel" technique—listen until their anger has no more fuel. 
*   **Ethics:** Virtue (Đạo) is the ultimate shield. If your character is solid, rumors (spies) cannot penetrate your reputation.

### 2. Family Governance (Ancestors & Children)
*   **The Command:** Treat children like "Young Shoots"—they need the right "Terrain" (Environment), not just "Discipline" (Force).
*   **The Elders:** Respecting elders is "Securing the Rear Guard." Their wisdom is your scout report for the future.
